Cosplay Competition

Important Guidelines

Read Before Registering
  • Each year level must provide at least 2 participants
  • One cosplayer per character only, duplicates are strictly prohibited. Contestants are encouraged to register early to secure their chosen character.
  • Contestants are allowed to cosplay any character of their choice, particularly game-related characters.
  • Contestants must attend all orientations and rehearsals scheduled by the organizing committee.
  • Qualified contestants will be officially announced via Email.
  • Background music must be submitted on or before the day prior to the event; failure to submit will result in using default music provided by the organizers.
